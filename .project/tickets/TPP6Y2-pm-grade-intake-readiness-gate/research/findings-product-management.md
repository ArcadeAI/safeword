# Raw findings: product-manager intake analogy

Raw web-search evidence. Synthesized into `intake-readiness-pm-analogy.md`.

## Cagan's Opportunity Assessment (the bounded core)

- **Marty Cagan / SVPG, "Assessing Product Opportunities"** — Opportunity Assessment is "an extremely important responsibility"; the challenge is to do it "in a quick, lightweight, yet effective manner." Full version is **10 questions** (not 4). https://www.svpg.com/assessing-product-opportunities/
- Worth running **even when the decision is already made** (e.g. CEO mandate) — value is being better-informed, not the gate.
- Cagan moved from heavy-weight PRD → lightweight high-fidelity prototype as the spec ("Lean Canvas vs. Opportunity Assessment", SVPG).
- For existing products it's "a very lightweight tool for highlighting the **risks** of the new effort."
- The load-bearing core distils to four: **(1) business objective** (why/outcome), **(2) how will you know if you succeeded** (success signal/done-state), **(3) what problem for the customer** (problem, not solution), **(4) who for** (persona). Source notes condensation: https://www.agile-minds.com/product-discovery-techniques-notes-on-how-to-create-tech-products-customers-love/ ; https://www.productcompass.pm/p/product-model-first-principles-transformed-cagan

## Supporting PM canon

- **JTBD** — "what job is the customer hiring this for?" Underlying motivation/desired outcome, not stated want. The XY-problem guard. https://zeda.io/blog/40-product-discovery-questions-for-product-managers
- **Four key discovery questions** — valuable, usable, feasible, strategic. **Cagan's four big risks** — value, usability, feasibility, business viability.
- **Teresa Torres, Continuous Discovery / Opportunity Solution Tree** — outcome → opportunities → solutions → **assumptions**; weekly customer-interview cadence; operationalizes Cagan. Discovery = assumption-hunting. https://www.mindtheproduct.com/teresa-torres-answers-your-product-discovery-questions/
- **Amazon Working Backwards / PR-FAQ** — write the press release (≤1.5 pages, + 3-4 pages FAQ) before development. End-state first. https://www.productcompass.pm/p/what-exactly-is-product-discovery
- **RICE** prioritization — Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort.
- **Clarify the metric before answering** — "when you say engagement, do you mean opens or click-through?" Disambiguate terms first. https://igotanoffer.com/blogs/product-manager/product-metric-interview-questions
- Senior PMs: ~60% strategic framing vs. execution.

## Net mapping to safeword

Safeword already extracts Cagan's four: `spec.md` JTBD (problem + who) against a personas file; `done_when` = success signal. The gate recognizes existing artifacts and adds the move both PMs and the military insist on: **"biggest unknown" → "riskiest assumption."** Caution: no PM brand-names (RICE/JTBD/OST) in gate copy — plain-English thinking prompts only.
